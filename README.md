# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing an e-commerce dataset to uncover insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, and delivery efficiency. The analysis helps identify key trends in revenue generation, customer purchasing patterns, product category performance, and delivery impact on customer satisfaction.

The dashboard was built using **Power BI**, while **SQL** & **Excel** was used for data exploration and KPI calculations.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze e-commerce data to support data-driven decision-making by identifying revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, payment preferences, and delivery performance. The goal is to uncover business opportunities for improving customer retention, optimizing product strategy, and enhancing operational efficiency.

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset used in this project is the **Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist**.

It contains information about:
- Orders
- Customers
- Sellers
- Products
- Payments
- Reviews
- Delivery details

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- The business generated **15.4M total revenue** from **99K orders**.
- **Health & Beauty** and **Watches & Gifts** are the top revenue-generating product categories.
- Revenue experienced a **significant decline in the 2018**, indicating possible seasonal or operational challenges.
- **São Paulo contributes the highest number of orders**, indicating strong regional demand.
- The **repeat customer rate is very low**, highlighting an opportunity to improve customer retention.
- **Delayed deliveries significantly reduce customer satisfaction**, with average ratings dropping compared to on-time deliveries.

---

## 📉 Business Problems Identified

- Low customer repeat rate
- Revenue fluctuations across months
- High dependency on a few cities for orders
- Delivery delays negatively impacting customer ratings

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Implement **customer loyalty programs** to improve repeat purchases.
- Improve **delivery time consistency** to enhance customer satisfaction.
- Expand marketing efforts in **underperforming regions**.
- Introduce **personalized offers and promotions** for high-value product categories.
